**"BETTER THAN ACR?" — DECONVOLUTION IS THE REAL UPGRADE (parked behind PR#47 validation + §11; advisor-vetted 2026-06-05).** Modern sharpening improves USM along **two independent axes**: (1) **halo suppression** (edge-aware: bilateral/guided/LLF) — we ALREADY have the guided-filter engine (He 2013, Texture/Clarity, sub-1% halo vs USM 580%), BUT that's **mid-scale LOCAL CONTRAST, a different job from fine-scale capture sharpening** — so the halo axis is solved for local-contrast, not wired into a sharpening role (and v10c's "LLF lost" was for the local-contrast role ONLY — says nothing about LLF for blur-recovery); (2) **blur recovery** (deconvolution: Richardson-Lucy/Wiener) — recovers the actual lens-MTF/AA/demosaic blur instead of faking edge contrast. **THIS IS THE REAL GAP — we have nothing here.** KEY: deconvolution serves the **FAITHFUL** path too, not just "exceed" — ACR itself blends USM↔deconvolution as Detail rises (per our own apply_sharpness docstring), so adding it makes us match the LRT JPG BETTER, not diverge (partly dissolves the faithful-vs-master split). CLEAN-ROOM (textbook, no GPL). **CATCH: gated entirely on PSF quality** — a guessed Gaussian σ gives "generic sharper-than-USM," not true lens-blur recovery; a WRONG PSF trades USM's halo for **ringing + amplified noise**; damped/regularized RL mitigates, doesn't eliminate ("recovers blur UNDER a PSF model; quality scales with PSF fidelity" — real tunable work, not a free win). **CNN is OUT — hallucination is a CATEGORY ERROR** (a learned model invents detail that wasn't captured, disqualifying for a recover-what-was-really-there / match-the-colorist pipeline; secondary: heavy at 24MP×thousands, not clean-room, temporally unstable). The true selection criterion = **smooth deterministic response** (a smooth fn of the input, no hard switches that toggle on noise frame-to-frame → no flicker, the cardinal timelapse sin; USM / fixed-PSF deconvolution / the soft-gated mask all pass, CNN fails — also justifies the existing smoothstep mask). **SPIKE when we return: a damped-RL pass with a fixed Gaussian PSF, measured on the demosaic battery's MTF50P + a ringing/noise check, BEFORE committing to PSF modeling** (see how much real acuity it buys over the USM).
